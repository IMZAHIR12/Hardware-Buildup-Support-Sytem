I'm looking to run video editing software smoothly. Can anyone recommend the ideal computer specs for seamless performance?

See Answers

Benjamin Williams

For smooth video editing, I'd recommend a computer with at least an Intel Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 processor, 16GB of RAM, and a dedicated graphics card like an NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1660 Ti or higher. Make sure you have a fast SSD for your storage and ample space. These specs should provide a seamless editing experience for most video projects.
